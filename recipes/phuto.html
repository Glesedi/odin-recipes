PHUTHO

Phutho is an african dish also called uphutho known as softpap

Phutho is a nice dish when served with amasi and sugar or milk

Ingredients

  • Maize meal
  • boiling water
  • salt
  1. boil your water
  2. Put your pot on a hot stove and add your boiling water
  3. Add your salt to your boiling water
  4. Pour your maize meal in the pot
  5. With your fork mix the mazie meal together
  6. Mix until the pap is fluffy
  7. Enjoy your Phuto with milk or amasi
